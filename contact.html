Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about L&G Foundation.

How can I make a donation to L&G Foundation?
You can make a donation via bank transfer to our account. The IBAN is BE68 5390 0754 4029 (BIC: GEBABEBB), held at BNP Paribas Fortis. The account holder is L&G Foundation. Please include your name and "Donation" as the transfer reference. All details are available on our Donation page.
Is L&G Foundation a registered nonprofit?
Yes. L&G Foundation is a registered Belgian Private Foundation (Stichting Private) with Enterprise Number 0899.387.265 and VAT number BE0899387265. We were officially founded on July 2, 2008, and have been operating continuously since that time. Our full legal details are available on our About page.
Can I apply for a community grant?
Yes. We accept grant applications on a rolling basis. Our review committee meets quarterly to evaluate new proposals. To apply, please contact us with a brief description of your project, including its goals, target beneficiaries, expected outcomes, and requested funding amount. We prioritize projects that align with our mission of heritage preservation, community welfare, environmental sustainability, and cultural education in the Knokke-Heist area.
How can I volunteer with L&G Foundation?
We welcome volunteers who can help with heritage documentation, event organization, environmental projects, and administrative support. To express your interest in volunteering, please send us a message through the contact form above or email us at wiliamgrog@gmail.com. We will match you with opportunities that fit your skills and availability.
How is my donation used?
We are committed to complete transparency. Approximately 30% of our spending goes to heritage preservation, 35% to community grants, 15% to environmental sustainability, 12% to cultural education, and only 8% to administration. We do not spend any funds on fundraising. Detailed breakdowns are available on our Impact page.
