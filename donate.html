Transparency Promise

We are committed to complete transparency in how we use your donations. Every euro received is allocated to our four core programs: Heritage Property Stewardship, Community Grants, Environmental Sustainability, and Cultural Education.

We publish detailed annual reports that show exactly how funds were allocated and what impact was achieved. You can review our financial accountability information on the Impact page. We also welcome questions about our finances and are happy to provide additional information upon request.

What Your Donation Funds

Here is how different donation amounts translate into real impact for our community:

€25

Provides educational materials for one heritage workshop, reaching up to 20 students.

€50

Funds the documentation and assessment of one heritage property at risk of neglect or damage.

€100

Covers the cost of native plant species for 50 meters of coastal dune restoration.

€250

Sponsors a community grant recipient for one quarter, supporting their local project.

€500

Funds emergency repairs on a heritage building, preventing further deterioration.

€1,000

Sponsors the annual Cultural Heritage Festival, providing free access for all community members.
