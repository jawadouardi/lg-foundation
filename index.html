Preserving Heritage, Building Futures

L&G Foundation is a registered Belgian nonprofit dedicated to preserving cultural heritage, supporting community welfare, and promoting sustainable development in Knokke-Heist, Belgium.

Make a Donation Our Programs

Our Four Pillars of Impact

We work across four key areas to create lasting positive change in our community and beyond.

H

Heritage Property Stewardship

We protect and maintain historically significant properties and landmarks in Knokke-Heist, ensuring that our architectural heritage is preserved for future generations.

C

Community Grants & Philanthropy

We provide financial support to local organizations and individuals who share our vision of a stronger, more connected community in the Belgian coast region.

E

Environmental Sustainability

We promote eco-friendly practices and fund projects that protect our natural environment along the Belgian coastline for current and future residents.

A

Cultural Education & Awareness

We organize educational programs and cultural events that celebrate our region's rich history and inspire community engagement in the arts and heritage.

"Our mission is to safeguard the cultural identity of Knokke-Heist while fostering community development through sustainable philanthropy, transparent governance, and meaningful partnerships."
